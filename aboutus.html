RADHIKA FOUNDATION


About Our Organisation

RADHIKA is a non-governmental organization based in Ghaziabad founded in the year 2025, working on an issue affecting the urban and rural mass, with a special issue on Education, Agriculture, Environment, Health & Women Empowerment. The Radhika is trying hard with the community – based efforts to improve the living condition of the deprived section of society. The Radhika team consists of dedicated professionals from diverse fields across Ghaziabad, united by a shared commitment to building a more informed, equitable, and compassionate society. Each member contributes their expertise to collectively support individuals and communities facing limited access to education, healthcare, resources, and emotional care—often termed “underprivileged.” Through focused efforts and a strong sense of responsibility, the team strives to create meaningful and sustainable change, offering hope, dignity, and new opportunities to those in need.

A Governing Committee consisting of 9 persons elected democratically by its General Body governs Radhika. Elections on the board are held every five years. Radhika runs on a fully decentralized management system headed by the Secretary, who is supported by a cadre of professional and administrative staff. Program and performances are monitored on a regular basis in quarterly review and planning meetings. Eminent experts from the field of management, agriculture, environment, health, women empowerment, and social work, etc. are invited to these meetings.

Organisational structure

Radhika strengthens its impact through partnerships that unite government, non-government, and corporate sectors to support socio-economic development, including agriculture, environment, health, and women’s empowerment. The organization also prioritizes staff development by regularly conducting need-based training programs

Vision

Our aim is to lead a transformative development movement that empowers marginalized and socio-economically weaker sections of society.

Mission

Our mission is to build a society where no individual is left outside the mainstream of development. We are committed to creating a community in which every person enjoys their human rights with dignity, equality, and respect.
